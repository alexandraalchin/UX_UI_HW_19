About Alex


I'm a experience and visual designer in the Denver area with a background in outdoor industry sales and management with a Certificate in UX/UI from Denver University. My strengths are in developing user personas and storyboards, wireframing & visual prototyping, and user interface development. I am motivated by the collaborative process, a skilled communicator driven by empathy and user-centered design. My time in the outdoor industry cultivated my love of development where I created training programs and have engagemened with a diverse team. I take an empathetic approach with all aspects of the design process and Maintain the customer’s perspective coupled with an eye for both visual design, copy and overall usability, I look to develop products that create meaningful solutions to its users and in turn grow a brand’s value within their industry.

If I am not home watching Bob’s Burgers and basting you can find me outdoors!
